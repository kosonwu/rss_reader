## Next steps to deploy

### Railway (Fetcher):
1. Connect your repo, set root directory to `app/fetcher`
2. Set env vars: `DATABASE_URL`, `FETCH_COORDINATOR_INTERVAL` (optional)
3. Copy the deployed URL (e.g. `https://rss-fetcher.railway.app`)

### Vercel (Next.js):
1. Connect your repo
2. Set env vars:
   - `NEXT_PUBLIC_CLERK_PUBLISHABLE_KEY`
   - `CLERK_SECRET_KEY`
   - `DATABASE_URL`
   - `FETCHER_URL=https://rss-fetcher.railway.app`  ← the Railway URL from above
